# Visionary

**Visionary** is the unified, intelligent product ecosystem developed by **VisionFX** — a specialist in visual effects and on-set data services for global film and television productions.

VisionFX empowers filmmakers and VFX teams by bridging on-set reality capture with post-production precision. Our work eliminates the "Human Error Gap" through automation, high-security tools, and consistent data workflows — ensuring faster delivery and 100% technical reliability on large-scale international projects.

## VisionFX Overview

VisionFX specializes in visual effects, LiDAR, photogrammetry, and on-set data acquisition for major film and TV productions. We operate where physical production meets digital post-production, delivering accurate, secure, and immediately usable data that powers breathtaking visual storytelling.

Key company projects include:
- **VisionFX Website** — Our public face, showcasing services, team credits (including One Piece, Indiana Jones, Resident Evil, and more), and production support.
- **VisionDrive™ Suite: The Intelligence Layer**  
  Our proprietary software suite designed to eliminate the "Human Error Gap" in the VFX pipeline. By automating the most tedious, high-stakes data tasks, we ensure faster delivery and 100% technical consistency across every stage.
- **Visionary Prep™ (AI Script Breakdown)**  
  • **The Intelligence**: An offline, high-security AI application that ingests production scripts to automatically identify required VFX assets, character cyber-scans, environment needs, props, locations, and more.  
  This tool transforms raw scripts into structured, actionable breakdowns — saving time, reducing errors, and accelerating pre-production and VFX planning.

Visionary extends these core innovations into a full cross-platform product family, making VisionFX intelligence accessible to VFX artists, supervisors, producers, and studios everywhere.

## The Visionary Product Family

Visionary is built as a modular, multi-platform suite centered around the VisionDrive intelligence layer and Visionary Prep AI capabilities.

- **VisionaryAPI** — Secure backend services powering asset identification, data consistency checks, and intelligence orchestration.
- **VisionaryWeb** — Modern web interface for script uploads, breakdowns, team collaboration, and real-time VFX planning.
- **VisionaryMac** — Native macOS application optimized for VFX supervisors and artists working in high-end post environments.
- **VisionaryWin** — Native Windows application tailored for pipeline integration in studio render farms and editorial suites.
- **VisionaryiOS** — Mobile iOS app for on-set reviews, quick asset lookups, and script annotations during principal photography.
- **VisionaryAndroid** — Mobile Android app providing the same on-the-go access for field teams and location scouts.

All clients connect to the shared **VisionaryAPI** for consistent, versioned intelligence — ensuring the same high-fidelity results whether you're on set, in editorial, or in post.
